# 📊 Customer Shopping Trends Analysis – India

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping trends in India using a complete Data Analytics workflow. The objective is to identify customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, product trends, and business insights through interactive dashboards.

The project demonstrates skills in data cleaning, SQL analysis, data visualization, and business intelligence using Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
- Identify top-performing product categories and brands.
- Compare Online vs Offline sales channels.
- Understand customer demographics and subscription behavior.
- Analyze regional sales performance across Indian cities.
- Generate business insights for data-driven decision making.

## 📊 Key Insights

- Clothing generated the highest sales among all product categories.
- Puma and Zara were the top-performing brands.
- Online and Offline sales remained consistent throughout the year.
- Non-subscribed customers contributed the majority of total sales.
- Pune and Chennai recorded the highest sales.
- Young Adult customers represented the largest customer segment.
- In-store purchases and Myntra generated the highest sales among shopping platforms.
